Journey Through the Cosmos

🌍

Our Home Planet

Earth is the only known planet in the universe to harbor life. It orbits the Sun at an average distance of 93 million miles and is the perfect distance for liquid water to exist.

🌙

The Moon

Earth's only natural satellite has been visited by 12 human astronauts. The Moon's gravity creates our ocean tides and has stabilized Earth's rotation for billions of years.

🔴

Mars Mission

The Red Planet is our next frontier. Scientists are working on missions to establish human presence on Mars, which could become humanity's second home in the solar system.

🛸

Space Stations

The International Space Station orbits Earth at 250 miles altitude, traveling at 17,500 mph. It's humanity's laboratory in space, hosting continuous research since 2000.

⭐

Distant Stars

Our Sun is just one of over 100 billion stars in the Milky Way galaxy. The nearest star system, Alpha Centauri, is 4.37 light-years away from Earth.

🌌

Beyond Our Galaxy

The observable universe contains approximately 2 trillion galaxies. Each galaxy contains millions to trillions of stars, and possibly countless planets waiting to be discovered.
